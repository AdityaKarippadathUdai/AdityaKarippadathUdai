## 🧠 About Me

I'm an AI-focused Computer Science student with a deep passion for designing systems that are not just functional, but architecturally sound and intelligent. My interests lie at the intersection of **AI/ML**, **backend engineering**, and **system design** — I'm driven by the challenge of understanding how large-scale systems are structured, how their components interact internally, and how they can be made both scalable and resilient. I've built full-stack and backend applications that bridge the gap between intelligent models and real-world interfaces, and I'm currently expanding my expertise in **Node.js**, **Linux**, and **DevOps practices**. My approach is rooted in systems thinking — I don't just write code, I architect solutions.
